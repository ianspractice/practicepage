Most Beautiful Places I've Ever Been

Remember, this is a personal preference

I'm from Uruguay and I have traveled to many countries.

  • Uruguay (of course)
  • Argentina
  • Japan
  • Canada
  • Spain
  • Switzerland
  • Italy

That being said, this is my personal top 5 most beautiful places that I have visited:



  1. Fuji Five Lakes Area

    One of the most iconic places in the world, and one of my favorite places. It's a peaceful place, and I highly recommend going if you visit Tokyo.

  2. Swiss Alps

    As you might notice, I love mountains, so the Swiss Alps have to be in my top list. Probably one of the most beautiful views in the world.

  3. 7 Lakes, Bariloche

    The 7 lakes of Bariloche have beautiful clear water and amazing views of the mountains.

  4. Iguazu Falls

    Iguazu Falls is probably the most amazing thing I have seen in my life. It's such a magical place.

  5. The Rambla of Montevideo

    This is my top one place in the world. Probably it's not the most epic or beautiful of the top, but it's my favorite because I was born there. There are all my memories.
